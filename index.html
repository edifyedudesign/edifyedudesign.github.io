Services

Edify helps growing technology and creative services companies to leverage learning to reach specific business outcomes. We offer three service packages as well as tailored services to meet your needs.

Seed Stage

At 10-50 employees, you know that your company needs effective managers and an understanding of what your employees need to get to productivity fast. Edify can help you close seed-level internal learning gaps even as you grow rapidly. We help you set in place the systems that will support fast-paced growth and innovation by delivering a manager skills assessment, business ecosystem audit, cost assessment for future learning programs, and a resource management plan designed just for you.

Growth Stage

With 51-150 employees, it's key to nail down learning and succession plans for employees, develop mentorship and subject matter expertise within the company, enact an efficient onboarding suite, and understand how the metrics of your learning programs help you reach your business goals. Edify can help you create these systems, hire the right team members, and manage the workflow for internal learning.

Build & Retain Stage

At 151-1000 employees, you know you're ready to solidify a culture of learning, a knowledge management philosophy, a serious strategy for connecting Learning & Development to performance, and a system for learning delivery across multiple offices and even countries. Edify can help you understand the world of the LMS, choose the right tools, manage change, and develop strategies for maintaining effective learning and knowledge management systems.
