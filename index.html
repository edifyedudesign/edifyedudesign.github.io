What is Edify?

We're a consultancy focused boosting the success of technology and creative industry companies with amazing learning experiences.

Our mission

We make effective, enjoyable learning that grows your organization.

Learner-Centric

We use a learner-centric approach to planning, building, testing, and refining learning experiences of all varieties. Edify focuses on adult learning through hands-on, instructor-led, self-managed, or online experiences.

We're specialists.

We bring cognitive science and experience design to employee onboarding, knowledge management, client education, and more. We help you leverage learning to achieve your goals no matter where you are or what you do.

Edify's Founder

Kristen Gallagher is the founder of Edify, a consultancy that designs and builds learning experiences that people actually learn from and like. She brings over eight years of instructional design, alternative learning environment experience, event management, business development, and nonprofit education experience to Edify. Recently, her focus has been on building educational experiences that empower people, especially young women and small business owners.

Services

Edify focuses on three specific learning & development areas to grow technology and creative businesses. We offer Discovery Engagements, Packaged Solutions, and Custom Consulting for each focus area.

Employee Training

Learning experiences including onboarding, orientation, internal training, and continuing education.

Customer Education

Opportunities for your clients and customers to learn more about you, your services & processes, and how working with you works.

Knowledge Management

Learning thrives on knowledge - so organizing and managing it is key; Edify helps set up and manage KM systems.

Process

Edify uses a learner-centric design philosophy in all of our work, be it training development, knowledge management, or education processes.
