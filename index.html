About Us


At Edify, we make learning experiences grow your employees, your customers, and your business. Period.

What is Edify?

Edify is a learning design consultancy focused boosting the success of technology and creative industry companies with amazing learning experiences.

Our mission

We make effective, enjoyable learning that grows your employees, customers, and business.

So?

We use a learner-centered approach to planning, building, testing, and refining learning experiences of all varieties. Edify focuses on adult learning through hands-on, instructor-led, self-managed, or online experiences.

We speak learning.

We bring cognitive science and human experience to employee onboarding, knowledge management, client education, and more. We help you leverage learning to achieve your goals no matter where you are or what you do.

Our Services

Edify focuses on three specific learning & development areas to grow technology and creative businesses.

Employee Training

Customer Education

Knowledge Management
