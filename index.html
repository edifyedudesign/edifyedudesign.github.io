Who Is Edify?

Kristen Gallagher is the founder of Edify. She brings over eight years of museum education, instructional design, alternative learning environment, and business strategy experience to Edify. Her focus is on helping businesses develop cultures of learning that empower both employees and raise the bottom line.

Gallagher teaches workshops, gives talks, and presents nationwide regularly. Her approach stems from an ecological basis of human organizational development and she strives to make every workplace a productive, healthy, and innovative environment.
